Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first book?

Open the BookAI app, tap the "+" button, enter your book idea or prompt, and let our AI generate your personalized book. You can customize the genre, length, and style to match your preferences.

Can I listen to my books as audiobooks?

Yes! BookAI automatically generates audio narrations for your books using advanced text-to-speech technology. You can listen to your books directly in the app.

How do I share my books with others?

Use the share button in your book library to generate a universal link. Recipients can view your book in the BookAI app or on the web, even if they don't have the app installed.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage. Your books and personal information are private and associated only with your account. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I edit or modify generated books?

Currently, books are generated as complete works. However, you can create new books with different prompts and parameters. We're working on editing features for future updates.

What devices and operating systems are supported?

BookAI is available for iOS devices running iOS 15.0 or later. Android support is coming soon. The app works on iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch.

Before You Contact Us
